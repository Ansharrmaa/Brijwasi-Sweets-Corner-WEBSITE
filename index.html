Our Story / हमारी कहानी

Welcome to Brijwasi Sweets Corner — a beloved family-owned sweet shop in the heart of Surajpur, Greater Noida. Founded by Sh. Ramdev Upadhyay, our shop has been serving the local community for over two decades with authentic, handcrafted sweets.

We are passionate about creating high-quality, mouth-watering sweets that you'll love. Our experienced chefs use only the freshest and finest ingredients — because at Brijwasi, every sweet tells a story of tradition, purity, and love.

"Sweets are not just food — they are an integral part of our culture and traditions."

  • Founded by Sh. Ramdev Upadhyay — 20+ years of excellence
  • Pure Desi Ghee used in every preparation
  • Fresh sweets made daily — no preservatives
  • Traditional recipes, authentic flavors
  • Wholesale & catering services available
  • Bulk orders for weddings, festivals & events
  • Custom gift boxes with dry fruits available
